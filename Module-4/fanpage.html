Bio

Led Zeppelin were an English Rock band formed in London in 1968, who over the course of 10 years, produced 8 studio albums in which they recieved great success. Since the group disbanded in 1980, the remaining members have periodically gotten together for reunion tours (incredible.) Led Zeppelin stands as one of most influential and innovative rock bands in history.
